We are a team of dedicated suppliers and hairstylists aiming to provide you the best place to express the love we share for synthetic dreads.

Is super important that everyone can be themselves, both in appearance and personality. And we want to do our bit for the world and the environment. By treating others the way we want to be treated. But also by offering products that make our customers more self-assured. Products that we have tested ourselves, but are also responsible and of high quality. We are busy with our work daily and grateful to be able to say that it still feels like a hobby.

  • Dreadlocks Shouldn’t Be Washed

  • You Can’t Sleep on Dreadlocks
  • You Have To Shave Your Head To Remove Them
  • Having Dreads is Bad For Your Scalp
